Cafe T-Rex is a very unique Restaurant. Not only do we strive to provide you with the highest quality food but we also ensure an experience that our customers will not soon forget. Cafe T-Rex is the perfect place for people of all ages to come have fun while filling your dinosaur-sized appetite. We have a very diverse menu which includes items such as our Bronto Burger, T-Rexadillas, and Layer of the Earth Lasagna. As expected everything comes in large portions, but don't let that scare you away from our mouth water deserts and appetizers.


Featured Items

Bronto Burger

A beef burger piled high with a fried onion ring. Served with lettuce, tomato, pickles and cheese.

T-Rexadillas

Chicken, roasted red bell peppers, green onions, Monterey Jack and Cheddar cheeses grilled in flour tortillas. Served with pico de galla and T-Rex avocado cream sauce.

Layer of the Earth Lasagna

Fresh pasta layered with tomato and meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, baked to perfection and served with a Caesar salad or mixed greens with your choice of dressing.
