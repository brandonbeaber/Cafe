About Cafe T-Rex


Cafe T-Rex offers our guest a family friendly environment which is tailored for all. The prehistoric environment makes it the perfect place for any event. We have a large arcade room as well as a variety of different attractions. We even offer private dining rooms which can be designed according to each individuals needs. When you come to Cafe T-Rex you can expect great food along with an experience you will not soon forget. We are committed to creating the best possible dining experience from our first contact with our guests through the end of the meal. We strive to provide all customers with an amazing overall experience at an affordable price. Call to schedule your next event with us!

About Our Food!

At Cafe T-Rex we have large variety of food. Our menu includes burgers, pizza, steak, fish, pasta, chicken, and desserts. With such a large variety everyone will surely find something that will suit their taste at a very affordable price. All of our food is prepared fresh daily by our experienced chefs.
